A useful Omega Infinity topology is translational_gap.

This is the pattern where mechanistic or genetic evidence can be compelling, but the bridge into clinical confidence is still incomplete. The key mistake is to treat biological plausibility as if it already survived contact with patient reality. Omega Infinity is strongest when it keeps that gap visible, because a signal can be real, interesting, and still not be ready for decision-grade trust. Good reasoning does not erase the gap just because the upstream story is elegant.
