1) Omega Infinity topology signal: translational_gap.

2) This appears when mechanistic and sometimes genetic evidence look strong, but clinical transfer is still unproven.

3) The failure mode is obvious once you see it: people mistake plausibility for portability.

4) A pathway can be coherent, a target can look biologically relevant, and the human-layer result can still remain unresolved.

5) Good decision systems do not flatten that uncertainty. They mark the gap.

6) In biomedical reasoning, the question is not only “does this signal support the hypothesis?” but also “did that support survive translation into patient-relevant evidence?”

7) If not, confidence should stay bounded.
